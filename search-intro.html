Is your search realistic?
Haven't checked your deposit ceiling yet? Start there → It calculates the maximum price your savings can reach after stamp duty and LMI — that's the budget to search with.
You pick a suburb and a budget. We tell you what share of properties actually sold there you could have afforded. Not vibes — real sales data.
NSW — 146,000+ individual sale records from the NSW Valuer General. Real prices, real addresses, real counts. This is as good as free data gets in Australia.
VIC — No individual sale records are publicly available. Scores are estimated using NSW market distribution curves matched to VIC suburb medians. Directionally useful, not exact.
All other states — Not yet. Individual sale records aren't publicly available for free anywhere else in Australia. Searching in QLD, WA, SA, or elsewhere? Let us know which state — we'll prioritise based on demand.
