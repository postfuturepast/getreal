In the works
Paying it off?

This tool is being built.

The idea is to track your property value and your mortgage together, and flag when something has changed enough that you should act.

It might be:

> Rates have dropped broadly and your bank hasn't passed it on

> Your loan-to-value ratio has improved and you're eligible for a better tier

> Your bank is offering new customers a better deal than you're on

The goal is simple. Keep the bastards honest.
